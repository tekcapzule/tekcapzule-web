Frequently
asked Questions

How can I subscribe to the TekCapsule training platform?

You can subscribe by creating an account on our website or app. Once you're logged in, click on "Subscribe Now" to select your preferred subscription plan and follow the prompts to complete the payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and select digital wallets. The availability of certain payment methods may vary by country.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use state-of-the-art encryption technology to ensure your personal information and payment details are always safe.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, log in to your account, navigate to the "My Account" section, select "Manage Subscription" and then choose "Cancel Subscription". Please note that cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before your next billing cycle to avoid being charged.

What types of AI training courses do you offer?

We offer a wide range of courses covering all areas of AI such as machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and more. These courses cater to various proficiency levels from beginners to advanced learners.

How often is the course content updated?

We strive to keep our course content as relevant and updated as possible. Generally, we review and update our courses every quarter.

Will I lose access to course material if my subscription ends?

After your subscription ends, you will lose access to the course materials. However, if you renew your subscription, you'll regain access.

What should I do if I'm having trouble accessing the platform?

First, try refreshing your page or restarting your device. If the problem persists, please contact our technical support team at contact@tekcapsule.com, providing as much detail about the issue as possible.

Is there any system requirement to use the platform?

Our platform can be accessed via web browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge on both desktop and mobile devices. For optimal performance, we recommend using the latest browser versions.

How interactive are the courses?

Our courses are designed to be highly interactive, with a mix of videos, quizzes, projects, and peer-reviewed assignments. Plus, you have the opportunity to participate in discussion forums and live webinars.

Is there a completion certificate for the courses?

Yes, upon completing a course and passing all required assessments, you will receive a certificate that you can share on your LinkedIn profile or with potential employers.

Can I pace my own learning?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed with flexibility in mind. You can learn at your own pace and on your own schedule.

How can I track my course progress?

Your course progress is tracked automatically on the platform. You can see it on your dashboard under the "My Courses" tab. It shows you the percentage of course material you've completed so far.

Can I take multiple courses at the same time?

Yes, you can enroll in and take as many courses as you like at the same time. Our platform allows for multi-course enrollment to accommodate varied learning interests.

Can I preview a course before subscribing?

We provide a preview of each course which includes an overview, course syllabus, and introductory video. However, to access the complete course materials, you will need to subscribe.

How can I change my account details?

To change your account details, simply log in and navigate to "My Account". Here you can update your name, email address, password, and other account information.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

If you've forgotten your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". You will be prompted to enter your email address. Once submitted, you will receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password.

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact our support team at contact@tekcapsule.com. Note that deleting your account will permanently erase your account details, course progress, and any certificates earned.

Are there opportunities for networking and collaboration?

Definitely! We have a community forum where you can engage in discussions, share ideas, and collaborate with other learners. We also host regular webinars and Q&A sessions with industry experts.

How can I report inappropriate behavior in the community forums?

If you encounter any inappropriate behavior or content in the forums, please report it using the "Report" button located on each post, or contact our support team. We take these reports very seriously and aim to maintain a respectful and inclusive learning environment.

Can I communicate with instructors or mentors?

Yes, our platform includes a feature for direct communication with instructors or mentors. You can ask questions, request feedback, or discuss course-related topics.
